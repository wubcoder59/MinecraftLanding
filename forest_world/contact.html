Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring my dog to Wilderness Haven?

We welcome leashed dogs on most of our trails, with the exception of sensitive wildlife areas such as the Woodland Observation Blind and Wetland Platform. Please keep dogs on a leash no longer than 6 feet at all times, and remember to pack out pet waste.

What should I bring for a day hike?

We recommend carrying water, snacks, weather-appropriate clothing, sturdy footwear, insect repellent, and sunscreen. A trail map, camera, and binoculars are also useful. Cell service is limited in some areas, so don't rely solely on phone GPS.

Are there camping facilities at Wilderness Haven?

Wilderness Haven does not currently offer overnight camping facilities. We do have partners within 10 miles who provide camping and lodging options. Contact our visitor center for recommendations.

Is photography allowed?

Photography for personal use is encouraged throughout Wilderness Haven. Commercial photography or filming requires a permit. Please be mindful not to disturb wildlife or other visitors while taking photos.
